What is rPlace?

rPlace is an app designed to collect opinions on Vermont pavement conditions from invited participants. rPlace will monitor where you travel and will ask you to rate the pavement of specific roads you have just driven on.

What condition are specific roads in? Given limited funding, how quickly would you like to see certain roads repaired? How often do you use these roads? rPlace automates the process of collecting this information, alleviates burden, and improves the overall quality of information delivered to the Vermont Agency of Transportation (VTrans).

Why should I participate?

VTrans will use information collected from this study to ensure they focus on repaving the right roads in the appropriate order across Vermont. As a participant, your involvement helps shape the outcome of these future plans—and the allocation of funding—by providing feedback on the roads you drive on.

How will my information be used?

As noted, the information gathered by rPlace helps direct future transportation plans and funding. rPlace includes robust privacy protections to protect your anonymity and privacy. Your data and information will not be sold. For more information, click here.
